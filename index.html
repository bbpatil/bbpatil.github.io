About Me

Mr. Basavaraj Patil is an Assistant Professor in the Department of CSE/ISE, SDM Institute of Technology, Ujire, Karnataka, INDIA. He is pursuing Ph.D. at Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. He received his M.Tech and B.E. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi in 2013 and 2010 respectively. He also received Teaching Assistantship under TEQIP 1.2 P.G Assistantship Scheme at SDMCET, Dharwad.

His research interests include Computer Networks, Wireless Sensor Networks, Cryptography, IoT, Cloud Computing and Image Processing. He has published around 10 papers in International and National conferences and Journals. He has delivered few invited talks, participated in number of workshops and organized state level and National level events. He is holding various academic responsibilities.

He is a professional member of Institute of Engineers (India), ISTE, Universal Association of Computer & Electronics Engineers (UACEE), International Association of Engineers (IAENGG) and International Association of Computer Science & Information Technology (IACSIT).
